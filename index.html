The Awesome World of Forza Horizon 4

Forza horizon 4 when it first came out was a truly incredible game that was loved by many. It was well loved for its incredible graphics, gameplay, and open world feel. This game allowed you to be free. If you wanted to fly down the road doing 200 miles per hour. You could do so. If you wanted to crash your car into a tree or a house, you could do so. This game allowed its players to be free and relase all of their anger and truly relax.
